Distribution Management System- DMS

  1. Home
  2. DMS

Associated with Synergy Interface Ltd.

A complete ERP system software with mobile application for the Salextra Limited, the mother distributor company of the Nokia mobile in Bangladesh.

Warehouse uploads the stocks regularly. Distributors can create orders for them and TSO/TSM can make orders for the distributors they are aligned with. Accounts panel check and review the order and approve it if everything is fine. Then the warehouse uploads the respective products for that order and delivers the order with the delivery information. After that the distributor can see his new products and receive them as primary purchase. Distributors sell the products to the retailer as secondary sales and then Retailers sell products to the customer as a Tertiary sales. All of these processes are monitored by respective cross checks.

When a retailer sells the mobile to the customer they active E-warranty for that mobile. And during this warranty period if the mobile faces any problem then the distributor and retail can claim the replacement for this and return it. Application and completion of these both are handled by this software as well.
